Tippy Tap - Study Space of the Month

1 December, 2017

Hey Guys! New space found! It's called Tap Coffee. How do you find it? Just follow the numbers on the door. Literally. Each coffeeshop has a different number, to signify the number of the store #genius. Some are bigger than others and therefore more spacious, some smaller but cozier.

What's Good?

  • Good coffee
  • Pretty space

    1 November, 2017

    This place is filled with natural light and quirky furniture. The study space of the month is the Wellcome Library. Having spoken to our fellow students we also discovered that not many know about it. So you are bound to find space to study in there (for now). Go ahead and give it a visit!
